Predictable Revenue

1 Revenue Operations & Execution

  • Owns the annual operating plan and divisional quota cascade — translating the board-approved number into channel-level commitments (direct field, indirect, retail, telemarketing, digital), then cascading each channel to team, weekly, and per-seller commitments with quarterly re-plans when macro or pipeline signals shift.
  • Runs a weekly revenue retro and forecast governance cadence as the single operating rhythm of the commercial org — lifting forecast accuracy to 95% with 3.3× pipeline coverage and turning pipeline review into a repeatable performance ritual.
  • Builds territory design, quota allocation, and capacity models — including GIS-integrated zoning that lifted confirmed market coverage from 62% to 89% in three months.
  • Designs compensation governance — partner commissions, sales comp plans, SPIFFs, and OTE structures as behavior-shaping instruments aligned to ARPA expansion, new-logo volume, NRR, and ARPU growth.
  • Operates funnel stage management and pipeline hygiene discipline — stage definitions, exit criteria, multithreading minimums, and late-stage management standards that keep the forecast credible.
  • Runs structured win/loss analysis programs — competitor displacement patterns, deal-stage drop-off root causes, and pricing sensitivity signals that feed back into product, pricing, and enablement curriculum.
  • Orchestrates Marketing, Sales, and CX alignment around shared revenue goals — common MQL/SQL/opportunity definitions, unified handoff protocols, and joint accountability frameworks.

2 GTM & Commercialization

  • Owns end-to-end product commercialization — translating strategy into detailed offer requirements, launch readiness activities, and post-launch governance across national, local, and private offers.
  • Runs launch readiness governance — cross-functional gating with marketing, finance, legal, product, sales, and operations; go/no-go decisions; and post-launch audit and financial reviews that keep execution honest.
  • Defines and governs offer eligibility, stackability, funding, and compliance — the rules, guardrails, and operating models that ensure execution consistency across channels and customer touchpoints.
  • Stands up deal desk and pricing & packaging governance — discount approval matrices, non-standard term reviews, and margin guardrails that accelerate complex deals without eroding unit economics.
  • Coordinates channel rollout across direct, indirect, retail, and digital — synchronizing content, pricing, eligibility, and incentives so the same offer behaves consistently wherever the customer encounters it.
  • Restructures product portfolios into needs-based GTM taxonomies and positioning — adopted as the standard across sales, marketing, channel partners, and digital self-service.
  • Manages in-flight offer changes and edge-case escalations after launch — impact assessment, financial review, comms plan, and clear executive ownership for every change.

3 Revenu Enablement & Readiness

  • Operates a Sales Plays library — dozens of concurrent Plays across product launches, VAS cross-sells, segment, channel, and industry motions, each with target cohort, value hypothesis, talk track, content kit, seller incentives, and measurable outcome — on a quarterly review and refresh cadence.
  • Embeds methodology fluency across the sales org — MEDDPICC for enterprise, Challenger for mid-market, and Command of the Message for SMB volume motions — codified into discovery banks, qualification scorecards, and manager coaching cards.
  • Builds tiered B2B sales academies and certification programs — 200+ frontline sellers and channel partners certified at 85% completion with 15% post-cert performance uplift.
  • Compresses frontline ramp time from 4 to 2 months through structured learning paths, certification checkpoints, weekly cadenced training, and manager-led reinforcement.
  • Runs manager-coaching cadences and reinforcement loops — drip content, microlearning, and coaching cards that turn one-time training into sustained behavior change.
  • Engineers PLM-embedded knowledge systems — a CMS gated as a launch step inside the product lifecycle process, governing fillable forms, customer presentations, product catalog, training, and Sales Plays assets — the single source of truth every rep, partner, and digital surface sells from.
  • Builds AI-native enablement content factories using CoPilot and Claude to generate lead discovery banks, objection-and-response libraries, competitive teardowns, field guidance kits, and certification quizzes at content velocity previously impossible.

4 Channel & Ecosystem

  • Stands up indirect channel programs end-to-end — recruited and enabled 5 SOHO partner firms with 100+ frontline agents delivering both Sales and Retention independently.
  • Builds partner enablement stacks — connected-tablet (CMS, CRM, Field App), co-sell playbooks, lead discovery, SOWs, and commercial bundles — enabling autonomous partner delivery at scale.
  • Develops strategic alliances and hyperscaler co-sell motions — Microsoft 365 attach playbooks that doubled M365 seats two years running, plus Google Workspace, Copilot, NotebookLM, IaaS, and security.
  • Runs GTM for a multi-vendor managed-services stack — Cisco Meraki, Huawei Smart Wi-Fi, SDN, managed routers, NGFW, WAF, CCTV-as-a-Service, and connected POS — turning carriers into one-stop digital partners across connectivity, networking, and physical/cyber security.
  • Creates new digital self-service and B2B2C embedded distribution channels — self-service and embedded sales motions in third-party customer journeys, cutting combined CAC by 20% and growing fiber market share at near-zero CAC.
  • Architects channel economics and partner operations — deal registration, co-sell workflows, attribution logic, and tiered commercial terms that scale indirect revenue without double-paying commissions.
  • Designs retention channels and post-sale customer ecosystems — renewal motions, win-back plays, and CX-led upsell paths that lifted partner NRR to 104% in a saturated market.

  • Deploys RAG-based AI assistants on GPT-4o integrated with CRM, regulatory tariff libraries, and product documentation — functioning as a 24/7 enablement assistant for customers, reps, and partners.
  • Architects and optimizes CRM and CMS integration across the commercial stack — data models, lead routing logic, pipeline stages, CPQ configuration, and automation workflows across MS Dynamics, Oracle SFA, and Siebel.
  • Redesigns end-to-end Lead-to-Cash workflows to eliminate revenue leakage and handoff friction — cutting order-to-activation from 9 days to 3 days.
  • Builds first-to-market B2B digital self-service platforms with guided CPQ, e-signature, and integrated RAG sales assistant — cutting digital order CAC by 50% and onboarding new customers in hours instead of days.
  • Builds executive dashboards and commercial analytics in Power BI, SQL, and Power Query — consolidating CRM, order, and territory data into a single source of truth for sales leadership and executive stakeholders.
  • Defines and operationalizes commercial KPIs — pipeline coverage, win rates, CAC, LTV, NRR, ARPA, ARPU, and time-to-close — translating raw performance data into actionable insights at every cadence.
  • Architects omnichannel data frameworks and revenue automation across orders, field reps, and retail shops — feeding CRM with current contacts, industry classification, and competitor intelligence. Outbound conversion lifted 18% → 25%.
